Why Bamboo

Bamboo bends. Blacktop shouldn't have to.

Asphalt that's left unsealed goes brittle fast — one hard freeze-thaw cycle and small cracks turn into potholes. A quality seal coat keeps the surface flexible enough to handle Alberta's swings, so it lasts longer instead of fighting the season.
